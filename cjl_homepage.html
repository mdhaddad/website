Our Mission

We believe in the transformative power of restorative justice to heal wounds, rebuild trust, and create stronger, more resilient communities.

Founded on principles of accountability, healing, and community empowerment, the Community Justice League works to address harm through dialogue, understanding, and collaborative problem-solving rather than punishment alone.

Healing-Centered

We prioritize healing for all affected parties

Community-Led

Solutions emerge from community wisdom

Justice-Focused

We seek justice that repairs and restores
