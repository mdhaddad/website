Our Approach

Building bridges where walls once stood

Community Justice League is a mission-focused volunteer-driven initiative to strengthen our neighbourhoods and build community resilience through restorative justice practices. We are neighbours helping our neighbours in dialogue and healing through community mediation circles.

We engage with individuals in various stages of the criminal justice system and offer them mentorship and supports in order to connect, build roots, and find purpose. Because reintegration doesn't work when individuals were never integrated into their communities to begin with.

We believe everyone is capable of growth and positive change such that we can reach our full potential, when given the right environment and support. Aside from providing mentors exemplifying model behaviour we will also look for apprenticeship opportunities in our community when appropriate.

With a referral we will also help victims and those they have offended reconcile and find common ground. We endeavour to provide legal services to our neighbours experiencing difficulties in the criminal justice system, where appropriate.

Mission

To heal our communities through dialogue and reconciliation, helping our downtrodden neighbours find purpose and meaning through mentorship, connection, and trust.

Vision

To realize our communities filled with hope, meaning, connection, and rootedness, such that no neighbour is a stranger and we all feel included.

Our Services

Four pillars of community healing

Mediation Icon

Mediation Circles

Facilitated dialogue sessions that bring together all parties affected by harm to discuss impact, responsibility, and paths forward in a safe, structured environment.

Mentorship Icon

Mentorship

One-on-one support connecting individuals with experienced community members who provide guidance, support, and opportunities for growth and integration.

Reconciliation Icon

Victim Reconciliation

Specialized support helping victims and offenders find common ground through carefully facilitated meetings focused on healing and understanding.

Legal Services

Accessible legal support for community members navigating the criminal justice system, ensuring everyone has representation and understands their rights.

Community Leader Image
"I see crime as sort of the tear in the fabric of community… it's just been torn, it leaves jagged edges. So then you kind of try to piece it back together, and with the needle and thread as you were to kind of allow the fabric of community be strengthen but it's not pretty; it's not like it was before… you can never undo what's been done but it gives you some sense that we as a community can do some patch work, patch things up.
