Our Approach

Building bridges through radical empathy

Community Justice League is a mission-focused volunteer-driven initiative to strengthen our neighbourhoods and build community resilience through restorative justice practices realized via integrated holistic services.

Our signature service is engagement with individuals in various stages of the criminal justice system to offer them mentorship and supports in order to connect, build roots, and find purpose. Because reintegration doesn't work when individuals were never integrated into their communities to begin with.

We believe we are all capable of personal growth and positive change such that we can reach our full potential and exhibit prosocial behaviour, when given the right environment and support. Aside from providing mentors exemplifying model behaviour we will also look for apprenticeship opportunities in our community when appropriate.

We are neighbours helping our neighbours in dialogue and healing through community mediation circles. With a referral we will also help victims and those they have offended reconcile and find common ground. We endeavour to provide legal services to our neighbours experiencing difficulties in the criminal justice system, where appropriate.

Mission

To heal our communities through dialogue and reconciliation, helping our downtrodden neighbours find purpose and meaning through mentorship, connection, and trust.

Vision

To realize our communities filled with hope, meaning, connection, and rootedness, such that no neighbour is a stranger and we all feel included.

Our Services

Four pillars of community healing

Mentorship

One-on-one support connecting individuals with experienced community members who provide guidance, support, and opportunities for growth and integration.

Learn More →

Mediation Circles

Facilitated dialogue sessions that bring together all parties affected by harm to discuss impact, responsibility, and paths forward in a safe, structured environment.

Learn More →

Victim Reconciliation

A voluntary process helping parties on both sides of a crime find common ground through carefully facilitated meetings focused on healing and understanding.

Learn More →

Legal Services

Accessible legal support for community members navigating the criminal justice system, ensuring everyone has representation and understands their rights.

Community Justice League KW operates on the traditional territory of the Neutral, Anishinaabeg, and Haudenosaunee peoples. Our community is situated on the Haldimand Tract, the land granted to the Six Nations that includes six miles on each side of the Grand River. Our active work toward reconciliation takes the form of advocacy for Land Back as well as community building and healing.
