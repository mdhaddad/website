What is Restorative Justice?

Restorative Justice is a philosophy and a social movement that views crime and conflict as harm done to people and relationships. It's a different way of thinking about justice, focusing on repairing that harm and rebuilding community trust.

Instead of asking "What law was broken, who broke it, and how should they be punished?", we ask "Who was harmed, what are their needs, and whose obligation is it to meet those needs?"
