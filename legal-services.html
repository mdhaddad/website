Legal Services

Navigating the Justice System with Community Support

Our legal services provide practical support to our neighbours experiencing difficulties within the criminal justice system. We believe that everyone deserves access to information, advocacy, and legal support regardless of their economic circumstances.

Legal Support Services Include:

  • Information and education about legal rights and processes
  • Assistance with navigating court systems and understanding legal documents
  • Support during court appearances and legal proceedings
  • Legal advice and representation in court where appropriate
  • Advocacy for restorative alternatives within the formal justice system
  • Connection to qualified legal representation when needed

Community-Centered Legal Advocacy:Our approach to legal services is rooted in our commitment to community healing. We work to ensure that individuals understand their options, including restorative alternatives, and help them make informed decisions that align with their values and circumstances.

Collaboration with Legal Professionals:We maintain partnerships with lawyers, paralegals, and other legal professionals who share our commitment to restorative approaches. This network ensures that community members receive appropriate professional legal representation when needed.

System Navigation Support:The criminal justice system can be overwhelming and confusing. Our trained volunteers provide practical support in understanding processes, completing paperwork, and preparing for court appearances, ensuring that individuals can participate meaningfully in decisions that affect their lives.
