Who We Are

The Evergreen Hope Foundation was born from a simple idea: that human well-being and environmental health are inextricably linked. We believe that by investing in local communities and restoring our planet's precious ecosystems, we can create a lasting legacy of hope, resilience, and prosperity.

From revitalizing forests to ensuring access to clean water, our work is driven by science, compassion, and a deep commitment to collaboration.
