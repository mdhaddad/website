Our Services

Community Justice League offers four interconnected services designed to heal communities and support meaningful transformation through restorative justice principles.

Mentorship

Building Connections, Roots, and Purpose

We pair individuals at various stages of the criminal justice system with trained community volunteers who provide long-term support, practical assistance, and genuine relationship. Our mentors help people develop roots in community, access resources, and discover their unique contributions through one-on-one guidance and apprenticeship opportunities.

Mediation Circles

Bringing People Together for Healing Dialogue

Our trained facilitators create safe spaces where victims, offenders, and community members can engage in honest conversation about harm and healing. These voluntary circles allow victims to be heard, offenders to understand impact and take responsibility, and communities to collaborate on repair and prevention.

Victim Reconciliation

Supporting Healing and Restoration for Those Harmed

We provide individual support and advocacy for those who have experienced harm, offering information about restorative options and facilitating dialogue opportunities when victims choose to engage. Our victim-centered approach honors each person's unique healing journey and never pressures participation.

Legal Services

Navigating the Justice System with Community Support

Our legal support helps our neighbours understand their rights, navigate court systems, and access qualified representation. We provide practical assistance with legal processes while advocating for restorative alternatives and ensuring individuals can participate meaningfully in decisions affecting their lives.

How to Access Our Services

Referrals Welcome From:

  • Courts and probation services
  • Legal aid organizations
  • Community agencies and organizations
  • Self-referrals from individuals and families
  • Healthcare and social service providers

Getting Started:

Contact us to discuss your situation and learn about available options. We begin with a conversation to understand needs, assess appropriateness for our services, and develop a plan that honors everyone involved.

Our Commitment:

All services are provided free of charge to participants. We believe that access to healing and justice should not depend on economic circumstances. Our services are confidential, culturally responsive, and grounded in respect for the dignity of all participants.

Ready to learn more about how Community Justice League can support you or someone you care about? Contact us to begin a conversation about healing, accountability, and positive change.
